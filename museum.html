Mission

At Dotsss, we spark play and discovery—not just for children, but for the communities that raise them. By bringing people together across backgrounds, we help children explore a world that's richer, more connected, and full of all possibility.

Dotsss is China's first independent children's museum, founded to explore new ways of supporting children's growth through creativity and collaboration. The name reflects our mission—connecting ideas, knowledge, and experiences to inspire exploration.

Destination Museums

Large-Scale, Comprehensive Family Venues. The expansive museums offer a variety of activities, making them ideal one-stop destinations for families. Children can spend an entire day or more exploring exhibits and attractions, providing an immersive and engaging experience for all ages.

Community Museums

Smaller, Flexible Spaces for Local Engagement. We bring high-quality museum experiences to local communities through smaller, adaptable community museums. These spaces are quicker to build, feature rotating exhibits, and provide creativity with an accessible, everyday way to engage with the content of larger destination museums.
