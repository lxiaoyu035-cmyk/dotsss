About

Dotsss Children's Museum & Dotsss Supplies

Dotsss is an independent children's museum built around discovery, play, and thoughtful community programming. Our retail arm extends that mission with products designed to feel useful, joyful, and easy to bring into real-world stores.

The result is a compact wholesale collection shaped for museums, family spaces, and gift environments: custom programs, ready-to-merchandise sets, and dependable everyday goods that support both revenue and experience.
